Research Interests

My research program advances integrative approaches to understanding and treating neuropsychiatric disorders, with a primary focus on Alzheimer's disease and cognitive decline. I combine multimodal neuroimaging (MRI, EEG), non-invasive brain stimulation (TMS/TBS), and computational modeling—including machine learning and connectome-based predictive approaches—to develop precision-targeted interventions and early diagnostic biomarkers. A central goal of my work is to bridge translational neuroscience and clinical application by identifying network-level mechanisms of cognitive dysfunction and leveraging them for individualized therapeutic strategies.
